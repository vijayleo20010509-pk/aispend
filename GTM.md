# GTM.md — SpendScout Go-To-Market Strategy

## Target Customer

**Primary:** CTOs and Engineering Managers at Series A–B startups (20–200 employees) who are spending $500–$5,000/month on AI tools and don't have a systematic process for reviewing those subscriptions.

**Secondary:** Founders of small dev teams (5–20 people) who personally manage SaaS spend and feel the pain of AI tool proliferation.

---

## Problem We're Solving

AI tool subscriptions have multiplied faster than procurement processes. Most teams:
- Don't know their total AI spend across tools
- Have multiple overlapping tools (e.g. Cursor + Copilot + ChatGPT)
- Are on plans sized for a team they had 6 months ago
- Assume API spend is fixed when it could be reduced via credit bundles

SpendScout surfaces these problems in 60 seconds, for free.

---

## Distribution Channels

### Channel 1: Organic / Word of Mouth
- Share URL feature: every audit generates a public link
- Founders share results on Twitter/X ("we were wasting $800/mo on AI tools")
- No friction: no signup required to run an audit

### Channel 2: Developer Communities
- Hacker News (Show HN post)
- Reddit: r/startups, r/SaaS, r/devops
- Indie Hackers

### Channel 3: Content Marketing
- "How much is your team actually spending on AI tools?" — blog post
- Benchmark data from aggregated (anonymized) audit results
- "State of AI tool spend" quarterly report

### Channel 4: Direct Outreach
- High-value leads (saving >= $500/mo) get personal outreach from Credex team
- Automated email with personalized savings figure drives reply rate

---

## Conversion Funnel

```
Visitor → Run Audit (no email required)
              ↓
         See Results
              ↓
         Enter Email (to receive report)
              ↓
         Credex team follows up on high-value leads
              ↓
         Credit bundle sale
```

**Key insight:** The audit result IS the sales pitch. Showing someone they're wasting $800/mo is more persuasive than any copy.

---

## Pricing Model

SpendScout audit tool: **Free** (lead generation for Credex)

Credex credit bundles: Margin on AI API credits (primary revenue)

---

## Launch Plan

### Week 1 — Soft Launch
- Share with 10 friendly founders for feedback
- Fix any critical UX issues
- Verify email flow works end-to-end

### Week 2 — Community Launch
- Post on Hacker News (Show HN)
- Post on relevant subreddits
- Share in Slack communities (Indie Hackers, SaaS founders groups)

### Week 3–4 — Iterate
- Analyze audit data for most common tool combinations
- Add tools requested by early users
- A/B test email subject lines for confirmation emails
