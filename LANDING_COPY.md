## FAQ

**Is this actually free?**
Yes. No credit card. No signup. Run as many audits as you want.

**How accurate are the savings estimates?**
Every number comes from official pricing pages (verified monthly). We link to sources in our methodology. We do not invent benchmarks.

**What do you do with my data?**
Audit results are stored anonymously. If you enter your email, we'll send you the report and follow up if we can help with credit bundles. We never sell your data.

**What are Credex credit bundles?**
Credex sources unused AI API credits from companies that over-provisioned, and resells them at 15–30% below retail. Same API, lower cost. We mention this only when your API spend is high enough to make it worthwhile.

**Can I share my results?**
Yes. Every audit generates a public link with identifying details removed.
